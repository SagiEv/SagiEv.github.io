// EXPLORE_04

AI-Generated Music Video

// J. Cole · Stable Diffusion · Generative Pipeline

Stable Diffusion Lalal.ai Uberduck AI Generative AI
// Description

Built a fully AI-driven production pipeline to generate a complete music video for a J. Cole track. The pipeline covered every dimension of the final product: visuals, voice elements, and audio processing — all generated from raw inputs using generative models.

Stable Diffusion handled the visual generation, producing consistent stylized frames across scenes. Lalal.ai was used for audio stem separation and processing, and Uberduck AI contributed to the voice synthesis layer. The final output is a fully synthetic clip assembled from AI-generated components, with no manual video footage used.
