Animated Music Video

// Tuna Clang · Motion Graphics · Visual Storytelling

Watch on YouTube Watch on Facebook After Effects Premiere Pro Adobe Animate
// Description

Produced a fully animated music video for the track Tuna Clang, combining motion graphics, frame-by-frame animation, and cinematic video editing into a cohesive visual narrative.

The creative focus was on visual rhythm — every cut, transition, and animated element was timed precisely to the music's pacing and beats. Used Adobe Animate for character and scene animation, After Effects for post-processing and compositing, and Premiere Pro for the final edit and color grading across the full track length.
